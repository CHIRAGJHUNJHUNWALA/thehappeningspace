How It Started

BUILT FOR THE COMMUNITY

On September 8, 2024, two friends from the same school, same batch, same city — decided to build something Surat had never seen before. Not just a dance studio. A space where passion has a home.

The idea was simple but powerful — build a community stronger than ever. A place where everyone who walks in grows with us, builds with us, and leaves feeling like they belong somewhere.

We come from business and marketing — not dance. But that's exactly what makes The Happening Space different. We built this the way a community should be built. With intention, with heart, and with everyone in mind.

"Learn, fail, make friends, spend the best time of your day at your second home."

This isn't a tagline. It's the reason we built this place. Every class, every workshop, every jam — it's all in service of this one idea.
